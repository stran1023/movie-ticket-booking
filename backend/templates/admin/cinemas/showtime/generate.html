🎬 Movie Configuration
📖 How to configure movies:
  • On (Checkbox): Only checked movies will be scheduled. Uncheck to temporarily exclude a movie.
  • Priority (1-10): Higher numbers mean the movie gets scheduled more frequently. (e.g., Priority 10 gets twice as many showtimes as Priority 5)
  • Time Slots: Choose which parts of the day the movie is allowed to play.
  • Base Price: The default ticket price for standard 2D formats. The system automatically adjusts prices for 3D/IMAX.
